2. Check the version of your Chrome Browser
✅Go to Settings, 
✅Then Click on About Chrome (Should be on the Left bottom side of the screen)
✅You'll get the version of your Chrome Browser (Mine is 87.0.4280.141)
3. Download ChromeDriver
✅Go to https://chromedriver.chromium.org/downloads
✅You'll see the list of available chromedriver
✅Download the appropiate version of ChromeDriver. My Chrome browser version is 87 so, I would download the one that matches my version.

4. After downloading the chromedriver.exe file for the respective OS and copy that .exe file into your local.
